STATE UNIVERSITY

In India, state universities are run and funded by the state government of each of the states of India. State universities are public universities run by the state government of each of the states and territories of India, and are usually established by a local legislative assembly act. The University Grants Commission (UGC), draws its power from the University Grants Commission Act, 1956. The latest list, published by the UGC on 4 March 2022, lists 264 universities as fit to receive Central/UGC assistance.
